# 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as **Task 3** of the **ApexPlanet Data Analytics Internship**.

The objective was to perform a **deep-dive business analysis** using the cleaned sales dataset and build an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, analyze customer behavior, identify revenue drivers, and explore business performance through dynamic visualizations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

To answer the following business question:

> **How can sales performance be improved by analyzing customer behavior, product performance, regional sales, and purchasing trends?**

The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making through interactive business intelligence reports.

# 📌 Key Insights

### Product Performance

- Laptop generated the highest revenue.
- Mobile closely followed Laptop.
- Shoes generated the lowest revenue.

---

### Category Performance

- Electronics contributed the highest revenue.
- Fashion generated the lowest revenue.

---

### Regional Analysis

- Patna recorded the highest revenue.
- Kolkata, Bengaluru, and Mumbai were also strong-performing markets.

---

### Customer Analysis

- Adult customers generated the highest revenue.
- Senior customers contributed the least.
- Revenue distribution between Male and Female customers remained balanced.

---

### Monthly Trend

Revenue peaked during:

- March
- June
- October
- November

Revenue declined during:

- August
- September

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in Electronics products.
- Launch campaigns in lower-performing cities.
- Improve customer retention through loyalty programs.
- Promote products during low-sales months.
- Target Adult and Middle Age customer segments with personalized marketing.
