# 📌 Project Overview

This repository contains **Task 2** of the **ApexPlanet Data Analytics Internship**, focusing on **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and **Business Intelligence (BI)** using the cleaned sales dataset prepared during **Task 1**.

The objective of this task is to analyze customer transactions, identify business trends, generate actionable insights, and support data-driven decision-making through Python, SQL, and data visualization.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Analyze business performance using Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Identify product, category, and city-wise sales trends
- Study customer demographics and purchasing behavior
- Perform Business Intelligence analysis
- Write business-focused SQL queries
- Generate strategic business recommendations

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Electronics is the highest revenue-generating category.
- Laptop, Mobile, and Book are the top-performing products.
- Patna is the highest revenue-generating city.
- Adult customers contribute the largest share of revenue.
- Customer revenue is distributed across a diverse customer base.
- March, June, and November are the strongest-performing months.
- Revenue contribution is balanced between male and female customers.
- Grocery records the highest Average Order Value.
