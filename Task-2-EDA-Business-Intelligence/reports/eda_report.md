# 1. Executive Summary

The objective of this Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was to examine the cleaned sales dataset, identify meaningful business patterns, evaluate key performance indicators (KPIs), and generate actionable insights through statistical analysis and visualization.

The analysis focused on product performance, customer behavior, geographic sales distribution, monthly revenue trends, and customer segmentation. These findings support data-driven business decisions and provide a foundation for Business Intelligence reporting and dashboard development.

# 3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Value |

### KPI Insights

The business generated approximately **₹139.40 million** in revenue from **992 unique orders** across **947 customers**. The Average Order Value of **₹140.52K** indicates relatively high-value transactions, while the Revenue per Customer demonstrates a strong contribution from the customer base.

---

# 4. Product Performance Analysis

Product-wise revenue analysis was performed to identify the highest-performing products.

### Key Findings

- Laptop generated the highest revenue.
- Mobile closely followed Laptop in revenue contribution.
- Book ranked as the third-highest revenue-generating product.
- Shoes recorded the lowest revenue among all products.

### Business Insight

The top three products contribute a significant share of total revenue and should remain the primary focus for inventory planning and marketing initiatives.

---

# 5. Category Performance Analysis

Category-wise analysis was conducted to evaluate business segment performance.

### Key Findings

- Electronics generated the highest total revenue.
- Education ranked second.
- Grocery followed closely behind Education.
- Furniture and Fashion contributed comparatively lower revenue.

### Business Insight

Electronics is the dominant business category and represents the largest opportunity for sustained revenue growth.

---

# 6. Geographic Sales Analysis

City-wise revenue was analyzed to understand regional business performance.

### Key Findings

- Patna generated the highest revenue.
- Kolkata, Bengaluru, and Mumbai also demonstrated strong sales performance.
- Pune and Gaya generated comparatively lower revenue.

### Business Insight

Revenue is well distributed across multiple cities, indicating a diversified customer base with opportunities for regional expansion.

---

# 7. Customer Segmentation Analysis

Customer segmentation was performed using Age_Group to understand purchasing behavior across demographic groups.

### Key Findings

| Age Group | Observation |
|-----------|-------------|
| Adult | Highest revenue contribution |
| Middle Age | Second-highest contributor |
| Young | Strong purchasing activity |
| Senior | Lowest revenue contribution |

### Business Insight

Adult customers represent the most valuable customer segment and should be prioritized in marketing and customer retention strategies.

---

# 8. Customer Revenue Analysis

The highest-value customers were identified using total revenue generated.

### Key Findings

- Top 10 customers contribute a significant portion of business revenue.
- Customer_335 generated the highest revenue.
- Customer_138 and Customer_266 followed closely behind.

### Business Insight

High-value customers represent important long-term business assets. Customer loyalty programs can help improve retention and maximize Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

---

# 9. Monthly Revenue Trend

Monthly sales analysis was performed to identify seasonal revenue patterns.

### Key Findings

- March recorded the highest monthly revenue.
- June and November also performed exceptionally well.
- August and September recorded comparatively lower revenue.

### Business Insight

Seasonal sales patterns should be incorporated into inventory planning, marketing campaigns, and revenue forecasting.

---

# 10. Gender-wise Revenue Analysis

Revenue contribution was analyzed based on customer gender.

### Key Findings

- Male customers contributed approximately 52% of total revenue.
- Female customers contributed approximately 48%.

### Business Insight

The balanced revenue distribution indicates that the business serves a diverse customer base without overdependence on a single demographic segment.

---

# 11. Average Order Value (AOV) Analysis

Average Order Value was calculated for each product category.

### Key Findings

| Category | Observation |
|----------|-------------|
| Grocery | Highest Average Order Value |
| Electronics | Second-highest AOV |
| Education | Strong transaction value |
| Furniture | Moderate AOV |
| Fashion | Lowest Average Order Value |

### Business Insight

Increasing Average Order Value through premium products, cross-selling, and bundled offers presents a significant opportunity for revenue growth.

---

# 12. Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are proposed:

### Product Strategy

- Prioritize high-performing products such as Laptop, Mobile, and Book.
- Improve sales performance for Shoes through promotional campaigns.

### Category Strategy

- Continue investing in Electronics.
- Strengthen Furniture and Fashion through targeted marketing.

### Customer Strategy

- Develop loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Focus customer retention efforts on Adult and Middle Age segments.

### Regional Strategy

- Expand marketing efforts in Patna, Kolkata, Bengaluru, and Mumbai.
- Improve regional campaigns in Pune and Gaya.

### Revenue Growth Strategy

- Increase inventory before high-performing months.
- Launch promotional campaigns during lower-performing periods.
- Encourage cross-selling to increase Average Order Value.

---

The Exploratory Data Analysis successfully transformed the cleaned sales dataset into meaningful business insights through statistical analysis and visualization.

The analysis identified key revenue drivers, customer segments, regional performance, seasonal sales patterns, and opportunities for business growth. These findings support data-driven decision-making and provide a strong foundation for Business Intelligence reporting, SQL analysis, interactive dashboard development, and executive reporting.
