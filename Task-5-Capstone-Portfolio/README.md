# 💡 Key Business Insights

### Product Performance

- Electronics generated the highest overall revenue.
- Laptop emerged as the highest revenue-generating product.
- Mobile recorded the highest sales volume.

### Customer Insights

- Adult customers contributed the highest revenue.
- Spending behavior remained statistically consistent across gender and age groups.

### Geographic Performance

- Patna generated the highest revenue.
- Kolkata, Bengaluru, Mumbai, and Hyderabad also performed strongly.

### Statistical Validation

- Strong positive correlation between Quantity Sold and Total Sales.
- No statistically significant spending difference across Gender or Age Groups.
- Statistical findings strengthened confidence in business recommendations.
