# 📊 Statistical Validation

To ensure that business insights were reliable, multiple statistical techniques were applied to validate analytical findings before drawing conclusions.

## Statistical Techniques Applied

| Statistical Test | Purpose |
|------------------|---------|
| Descriptive Statistics | Summarize numerical features |
| Distribution Analysis | Understand sales distribution |
| Outlier Detection (IQR) | Detect extreme observations |
| Pearson Correlation | Measure relationship between Quantity Sold and Total Sales |
| Independent Sample T-Test | Compare customer spending across genders |
| One-Way ANOVA | Compare spending across age groups |
| Confidence Interval | Estimate the population mean of Total Sales |

---

# 📈 Statistical Findings

## Pearson Correlation

**Variables**

- Quantity Sold
- Total Sales

**Results**

- Correlation Coefficient: **0.6466**
- P-value: **< 0.001**

### Business Insight

A strong positive relationship exists between Quantity Sold and Total Sales, indicating that increasing product volume directly contributes to revenue growth.

---

## Independent Sample T-Test

**Comparison**

- Male vs Female Customer Spending

**Results**

- P-value: **0.4950**

### Business Insight

No statistically significant difference exists in customer spending between male and female customers.

---

## One-Way ANOVA

**Comparison**

- Young
- Adult
- Middle Age
- Senior

**Results**

- P-value: **0.9727**

### Business Insight

Average spending remains statistically similar across all customer age groups.

---

## 95% Confidence Interval

**Mean Total Sales**

- Lower Bound: **₹132,319**
- Upper Bound: **₹146,480**

### Business Insight

The true average order value is expected to lie within this interval with 95% confidence.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Total Revenue reached **₹139.40 Million**.
- Electronics generated the highest category revenue.
- Laptop generated the highest revenue among products.
- Mobile recorded the highest quantity sold.
- Grocery achieved the highest Average Order Value.
- Patna emerged as the highest revenue-generating city.
- Revenue experienced seasonal declines during August and September.
- Customer spending showed no statistically significant differences across gender or age groups.

---

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Prioritize Electronics inventory management.
- Promote Laptop and Mobile as flagship products.
- Increase Average Order Value through cross-selling and product bundles.
- Implement customer loyalty and retention programs.
- Focus marketing investment on high-performing cities.
- Improve Fashion category performance through pricing and promotional strategies.
- Launch seasonal campaigns during low-performing months.
- Utilize Power BI dashboards for continuous business monitoring.
