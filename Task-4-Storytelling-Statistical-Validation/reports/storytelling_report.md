# Sales Analytics Storytelling Report

## ApexPlanet Data Analytics Internship

---

# Executive Summary

This project transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through exploratory analysis, statistical validation, and interactive dashboarding.

The analysis identifies key revenue drivers, customer purchasing patterns, regional performance, and product trends to support strategic decision-making.

---

# Business Objective

The primary objective was to answer the following business questions:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which customer segments contribute the most?
- Which cities perform best?
- What trends exist throughout the year?
- Which KPIs should management monitor?

---

| KPI | Value |

---

# Revenue Story

Electronics generated the highest category revenue, contributing approximately ₹50.8 Million.

Laptop and Mobile products emerged as the strongest individual revenue generators.

Monthly revenue remained stable throughout most of the year, with noticeable declines during August and September before recovering in the final quarter.

---

# Customer Story

Customer purchasing behavior remained consistent across gender and age groups.

Adult customers contributed the largest share of revenue, while Senior customers generated comparatively lower sales.

A small group of high-value customers contributed significantly to total revenue, highlighting opportunities for customer retention programs.

---

# Geographic Story

Patna recorded the highest revenue among all cities.

Kolkata, Bengaluru, and Mumbai also demonstrated strong business performance.

Regional insights suggest opportunities for expanding marketing activities in high-performing markets.

---

# Product Performance

Key findings include:

- Electronics was the leading product category.
- Grocery achieved the highest Average Order Value.
- Fashion generated comparatively lower revenue.
- Mobile recorded the highest quantity sold.

These insights can guide inventory planning and promotional strategies.

---

### Customer & Sales Deep Dive

---

# Statistical Validation

Statistical testing confirmed that:

- Quantity Sold significantly influences revenue.
- Unit Price strongly impacts Total Sales.
- Customer gender and age group do not significantly affect purchasing behavior.

These findings strengthen confidence in the business recommendations.

---

# Strategic Recommendations

- Expand Electronics product offerings.
- Increase Average Order Value through bundled promotions.
- Launch targeted campaigns during low-performing months.
- Develop customer loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Continue investing in high-performing regional markets.

This project successfully demonstrates an end-to-end Sales Analytics workflow encompassing data preparation, exploratory analysis, statistical validation, business intelligence, and dashboard development.
